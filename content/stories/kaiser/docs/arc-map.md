# Arc Map

What is written, and what is still open. **Nothing beyond Ch5 is planned.** Plan each chapter as you
reach it. Physical truth must stay consistent with [Power System](power-system.md).

## Four tracks

Every chapter should move at least one, and **every technical victory must worsen another.**

| Track | What it measures |
|------|------|
| Technical | what Kaiser can read, model, or build |
| Social | how others read him, and what that costs him |
| Moral | what he owes, refuses, or is willing to spend |
| Information | what he knows, what others know about him, and the gap |

## Written — Ch1 to Ch5

**The old world gets one chapter.** Purpose: prove Kaiser is excellent, then let Anakara price that
at nothing and kill him with it. No mentor, no bond, no violent death.

- [Ch1 — Anakara](../chapters/chapter1.mdx): a boiler repair and a ration run prove his competence.
  Then the acid air gives him **Sarpic**. The cure exists but needs gold-tier insurance, which only
  an employer can open, and he is registered to nobody. He dies in his sleep, alone.
- [Ch2 — Sis Diyarı](../chapters/chapter2.mdx): the Mist takes what belonged to him alone and cannot
  touch what belongs to nobody. Names, faces and his life go; knowledge and method stay.
- [Ch3 — Uyanış](../chapters/chapter3.mdx): he wakes wounded beside a wrecked caravan. Survival
  before explanation; a distant cold light is the first external question.
- [Ch4 — Kovuk](../chapters/chapter4.mdx): two days under the wagon, recovery as an engineering
  problem. His unworn hands and the foreign cargo open mysteries without solving any.
- [Ch5 — Ölü Bileşen](../chapters/chapter5.mdx): he reads the caravan as a targeted attack and takes
  apart a crystal lantern. A dim cell and a spent one give him his first testable model.

**Exit state.** He can walk but is not healed. He carries salvaged food, cloth, rope, knives, coins,
boots, one dim blue crystal, its lantern frame, and a damaged comparison housing. He knows the
crystal holds a finite store. He does not know mana, casting, the caravan's target, or the watcher.

## Open threads

Use them or retire them deliberately.

- **The watcher.** Someone saw him handle a failing crystal without the expected ritual.
- **The crystal.** Finite store, trigger unknown, orientation matters. Recharging untested.
- **The raid.** Cargo untouched, so the target was a person or one object. He suspects himself.
- **One corpse burned from the inside.** Unexplained.
- **The body.** His hands have never worked; he cannot say how old he is.
- **The clothes.** Hand-stitched, foreign, a perfect fit, and he does not remember being dressed.
- **The name.** *Kaiser* points to nobody yet.

## Hidden canon

Kaiser does not know this and the reader must not be told. He is **Ocak Kanlı**: his body channels
mana from the Mist on its own. It explains why his spark ignited instead of dissolving (Ch2). See
[Characters](characters.md).
