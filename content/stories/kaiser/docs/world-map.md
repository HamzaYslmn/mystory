Text transcription of the world map image so an AI can reason about geography
without seeing the picture. The map has no labels; names below are placeholders

other (compass directions), not by coordinates.

## Overview

A single ocean world. One large continent in the center-west, a smaller arid
landmass to its south (near-connected by a narrow strait), an island chain to
the northeast, a wall of permanent ice across the entire north, and scattered
small islands. Deep dark ocean everywhere else, with a visible current-gyre in
the southeast waters.
